Curry

A delicious, affordable, and simple-to-execute curry recipe you can warm your stomach and heart with.

Ingredients

  • Golden Curry cubes (mild, medium, or spicy)
  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled
  • 2 large carrots, peeled
  • 2 handfuls of mushrooms, chopped
  • 3 spring onions, finely chopped
  • A dash of Chinese five spice

Steps

  1. Chop the veggies.
  2. Stir-fry the veggies.
  3. Put the veggies in a pot and barely cover over with water.
  4. Put heat on high and get to a boil.
  5. After reaching a boil, cover with lid and reduce to low heat for 15 minutes.
  6. In the meantime: finely chop two curry cubes.
  7. After the 15 minutes is up, add the chopped curry cubes to the pot. Stir continuously for 5 minutes. Add a dash of Chinese five spice.
  8. Serve over rice, add in a fried tortilla, use as a dip for naan - the possibilities are endless!
