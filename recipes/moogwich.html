Moogwich

Marilyn's signature sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Seeded wheat bread
  • Butter
  • Violife cheese slices
  • Tofurky slices (Hickory Smoked Deli is a favorite of mine!) or other faux meat slices of your choice
  • Pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Sriracha

Steps

  1. Toast and butter the bread.
  2. Apply to each side in this order: 1 slice of cheese, 1 slice of Tofurky, a handful of lettuce, 2 pickles, and a splash of sriracha sauce.
  3. Close the sandwich and eat up!
